More about my passion project

  • Pickle Wagon is an anonymous social media platform and resource for individuals recovering from substance abuse. My background in mental health and substance abuse, combined with my passion for software development, led me to create this platform. Through my years of working in the field, I saw a clear need for an accessible and user-friendly resource that could make the process of seeking help for substance abuse easier. I wanted to bridge this gap by leveraging my technical skills to create a platform that connects those in need with the resources and support they need.

  • Pickle Wagon is a community of support for individuals navigating the lifelong journey of recovery. My platform provides a safe and anonymous space for individuals to share their struggles and connect with others who understand what they're going through. I also offer a space for users to discuss and review specific treatment facilities, allowing others to make more informed decisions about their care. I believe it's critical to have open and honest conversations about the realities of substance abuse and the challenges of recovery- rather than sugarcoating or stigmatizing the topic. The goal is to empower individuals in recovery by connecting them with the resources and support they need to achieve lasting sobriety.

  • This platform is a work in progress, and it will continue to improve and change just as the world does.
